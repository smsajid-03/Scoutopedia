Serious Mode - Scout Questions

Camping

Q: What is the first rule of campfire safety?
A: Always keep water nearby and never leave fire unattended.
Q: How do you properly pitch a tent?
A: Choose a flat area, stake corners tightly, and secure guy lines.

Knots

Q: Which knot is used to tie two ropes together?
A: Square knot (reef knot).
Q: Which knot is used to make a fixed loop?
A: Bowline knot.
